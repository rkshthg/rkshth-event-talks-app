WORLD NEWS REPORT - JUNE 16, 2026

1. GLOBAL POLITICS & CONFLICT
- US-Iran Peace Deal: President Donald Trump, currently attending the G7 summit in France, is touting a preliminary peace deal with Iran expected to be finalized this week. - Israel's Stance: Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu expressed concerns regarding the deal, emphasizing that Israel remains committed to preventing Iran from acquiring nuclear weapons regardless of the agreement.
- EU Sanctions on Russia: The European Union has adopted a new package of sanctions targeting Russia's "shadow fleet," military capabilities, and energy revenues, to increase pressure over the ongoing war in Ukraine.
- Post-Conflict Focus: President Trump has indicated his intention to seek an end to the fighting in Ukraine and Lebanon following the finalization of the Iran deal.
